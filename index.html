    South Indian foods

    Idli and Dosa are traditional South Indian foods, which are also popular all over India. The only way to get the best idli's is to have perfectly fermented batter. Idli is a savory rice cake, made by steaming a batter consisting of lentils and rice. Dosa is a crispy crepe made with the same batter of lentils and rice
